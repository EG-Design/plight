ZERON

Zeron, generally refers to the universe as a whole. Zeron itself is poorly estimated to be ~10000 years old, only the last 1-2 thousand years are "properly" recorded. In current times there are three factions battling over planets and resources (MV, Advanced Corporations and Kal Agency). Each faction seeks power but does not want to overextend and let one of their rivals strike them when they are weak. Star craft exist but small craft are not reliable for interstellar travel. There is a miracle energy extracted from crystals (ENG), it is what powers most technology, but it sap all magic for unknown reasons.

RHUNE

If Zeron is the crust of the planet, Rhune is the mantle, a nervous system under the skin. Rhune barely spoken about, and even then it is considered a myth or legend. An idea of which most never take into account, never taking the time to consider the reality which lies beneath their feet.

FACTIONS

Alomori

Alomori seek to help the less fortunate, they are a "peaceful" faction mainly consisting of Fayrish they set up encampments surrounding the outskirts battlefields, treating wounded who run from battle, or left behind. They don't do such deeds out of kindness, but rather to experiment with new medical techniques and tests, some which go less than ideal, seeing a Alomori member could mean rescue and healing or a fate worse than you would find on the battlefield.

Advanced Corporations

Advanced Corporations, one of the three rivaling factions. Advanced is the most formal of the three, have a sleek blue and silver uniforms, standard battle vehicles, and powerful sets of armor. Most of Advanced are human.

Eevee

Eevee, a superpower that fought against three factions at once, finally taken down by the might of the Eiraditch army they had technologies beyond anything ever seen. Having legions of soldiers armed with ENGR weapons, heavy star fighters and frigates, along with long range scanners made their attacks devastating and unpredictable.

Eiraditch Syndicate

All Eiraditch are members of the Eiraditch Syndicate, it being a syndicate the faction is not a large organization but rather it assembles when needed. Even though they are still recovering from the war with Eevee, none want to anger the Eiraditch

Hipyr

Hipyr, an undercover organization that used to fuel eevee's armada. They now support Advanced Corporations, or so it seems. Hipyr has some of the greatest minds in the industry and even though the organization's size itself may be underwhelming their strange tech has proven a great threat against those who seek to uncover them.

Kal Agency

Kal Agency, one of the three rivaling factions mostly consiting of Antyshoal. Unlike the others Kal Agency does not use ENG stims, instead, their field "medics" use pages from spellbooks to repair battle damage. Their use of orange magic has proven devastating in war and impressive in industry.

Knights of Kerauno

The Knights of Kerauno is a Veodian faction. Mostly unheard of but rising in power quickly, Veodians are still quite new to Zeron but their focus on star core based tech has created unique powerful technologies and sonic weapons. Since star cores are so rare most could not sustain such tech, but the Knights of Kerauno are based of a galaxy on the rim of Zeron, where star cores are more common.

Maltech Vestus

MV or Maltech Vestus, one of the three rivaling factions. MV is a conglomeration of different races, magic users and tech alike the only way to recognize members are their welding mask like helmets, yet that is not a sure sign.

TECH

ENG/Crystals

"Energy" or ENG is a strange substance made from giant crystals commonly found in vast caves on moons and deep under some planets. It's properties make it suitable for a weapon propellent, fuel for both land vehicles and starcraft, explosives, it can harden metal alloys to a great degree and can be converted into power without much loss. However it comes at a cost, as it saps magic users greatly for unknown reasons.

Star cores

In the depths of space there are orbs of powerful energy, that differs from the energy found in the crystals. Star cores come in a few sizes and colors: White when very small, light blue, the blue and then a dark blue followed by black when they implode. Since they last for thousands of years the implosions are not much of an issue. They power the star frigates, but can be used to power machinery and even weapons, since they are so rare it is not feasible for most to use them this way.

Powder and Gas

Gunpowder and Gas are rarer substances in Zeron, not because they are hard to make, but since the ENG mines and factories were founded few try to make these lesser used (and less efficient) materials. Magic users do use powder weapons, most notably the Antyshoal field medics.

RACES

Fayrish

Bird/moth-like “fairies” that use bio-tech and wear mask similar to plague doctors. Very good and healing stuff but all are born ill with an incurable disease.

Aeri

Giant wolves wielding giant swords. Mostly all primitive and tribal but there are a few in civilization.

Silmari

Aquatic but able to breath on land. Silmari have shells for heads that they pass down for generations. They have steam powered tech powered from underwater vents.

Yarian

Lizard folk. Very susceptible to diseases they prefer to stay away From all other races other than Fayrish. They have large castles and medieval weapons.

Eiraditch

Human like apart from digitigrade legs and odd gemlike eyes, Eiraditch are almost indestructible and are huge in numbers. They thrive from war but are still recovering from the last. They use meele polearms, a ranged weapon is shameful.

Antyshoal

Black fur and canine-like. In the top three most common races. They wear strange white masks and rarely (if ever) expose there faces.

Veodian

Cyborg lizards. They are rising in power but still little in number, not much is known of them by other races. They have star core tech along with sonic weapons.

Coromoth

Triangle headed “robots” created by the Eiraditch for a war. Heard to have organic wiring, and much more sentient than any other robot thus why they are deemed a race. Coromoth are hive minded, they have the capacity to make individual decisions but all interconnected through a network.

Human

Somehow found there way into Zeron, now they are one of the most common races, and by far the most common in cities. Most humans in Zeron focus on tech, but there are a few who study magic.
