RHUNE

If Zeron is the crust of the planet, Rhune is the mantle. The nervous system under the skin. Rhune is considered a myth or legend to most. A concept of an idea of which most never take into account, never taking the time to consider the reality which lies just beneath their feet.

FACTIONS

Alomori

Alomori seek to help the less fortunate, they are a peaceful faction mainly consisting of Fayrish they set up encampments surrounding the outskirts battlefields, taking in those who will not be able fight again. They don't do such deeds out of kindness, but rather to experiment with new medical techniques and tests, some which go less than ideal, seeing a Alomori member could mean rescue or a fate worse than death.

Advanced Corporations

Advanced Corporations, one of the three rivaling factions. Advanced is the most formal of the three, have a sleek blue and silver uniform and standard battle vehicles.

TECH

ENG/Crystals

"Energy" or ENG is a strange substance made from giant crystals commonly found in vast caves on moons and deep under some planets. It's properties make it suitable for a weapon propellent, fuel for both land vehicles and starcraft, explosives, it can harden metal alloys to a great degree and can be converted into power without much loss. However it comes at a cost, as it saps magic users greatly for unknown reasons.

Star cores

In the depths of space there are orbs of powerful energy, that differs from the energy found in the crystals. Star cores come in a few sizes and colors: White when very small, light blue, the blue and then a dark blue followed by black when they implode. Since they last for thousands of years the implosions are not much of an issue. They power the star frigates, but can be used to power machinery and even weapons, since they are so rare it is not feasible for most to use them this way.

Powder and Gas

Gunpowder and Gas are rarer substances in Zeron, not because they are hard to make, but since the ENG mines and factories were founded few try to make these lesser used (and less efficient) materials. Magic users do use powder weapons, most notably the Antyshoal field "medics".

RACES

Fayrish

Bird/moth-like “fairies” that use bio-tech and wear mask similar to plague doctors. Very good and healing stuff but all are born ill with an incurable disease.

Aeri

Giant wolves wielding giant swords. Mostly all primitive and tribal but there are a few in civilization.

Silmari

Aquatic but able to breath on land. Silmari have shells for heads that they pass down for generations. They have steam powered tech powered from underwater vents.

Yarian

Lizard folk. Very susceptible to diseases they prefer to stay away From all other races other than Fayrish. They have large castles and medieval weapons.

Eiradicth

Human like apart from digitigrade legs and odd gemlike eyes, Eiraditch are almost indestructible and are huge in numbers. They thrive from war but are still recovering from the last. They use meele polearms, a ranged weapon is shameful.

Antyshoal

Black fur and canine-like. In the top three most common races. They wear strange white masks and rarely (if ever) expose there faces.

Veodian

Cyborg lizards. They are rising in power but still little in number, not much is known of them by other races. They have star core tech along with sonic weapons.

Coromoth

Triangle headed “robots” created by the Eiraditch. Heard to have organic wiring, and much more sentient than any other robot thus why they are deemed a race.

Human

Somehow found there way into Zeron, now they are one of the most common races, and by far the most common in cities. Most humans in Zeron focus on tech, but there are a few who study magic.
