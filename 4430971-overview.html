ZERON

Zeron, generally refers to the universe as a whole. Zeron itself is poorly estimated to be ~10000 years old, only the last 1-2 thousand years are "properly" recorded. In current times there are three factions battling over planets and resources (MV, Advanced Corporations and Kal Agency). Each faction seeks power but does not want to overextend and let one of their rivals strike them when they are weak. Star craft exist but small craft are not reliable for interstellar travel. There is a miracle energy extracted from crystals (ENG), it is what powers most technology, but it sap all magic for unknown reasons.

RHUNE

Rhune is the mantle, a nervous system. Rhune itself is barely spoken about, and even then it is considered a myth or legend. An idea of which most never take the time to consider which the reality that lies beneath their feet. "Rhunes" refer to the a scripting language used in magic, it can be written, spoken, etched into material, even played into eerie songs with music. The curious thing is that even those who know how to use Rhunes don't understand what they mean, Rhunes seem to change and warp even as one uses them.

FACTIONS

Alomori

Alomori seek to help the less fortunate, they are a "peaceful" faction mainly consisting of Fayrish they set up encampments surrounding the outskirts battlefields, treating wounded who run from battle, or left behind. They don't do such deeds out of kindness, but rather to experiment with new medical techniques and tests, some which go less than ideal, seeing a Alomori member could mean rescue and healing or a fate worse than you would find on the battlefield.

Advanced Corporations

Advanced Corporations, one of the three rivaling factions. Advanced is the most formal of the three, have a sleek blue and silver uniforms, standard battle vehicles, and powerful sets of armor. Most of Advanced are human.

Eevee

Eevee, a superpower that fought against three factions at once, finally taken down by the might of the Eiraditch army they had technologies beyond anything ever seen. Having legions of soldiers armed with ENGR weapons, heavy star fighters and frigates, along with long range scanners made their attacks devastating and unpredictable.

Eiraditch Syndicate

All Eiraditch are members of the Eiraditch Syndicate, it being a syndicate the faction is not a large organization but rather it assembles when needed. Even though they are still recovering from the war with Eevee they still pose the greatest threat in Zeron.

Hipyr

Hipyr, an undercover organization that used to fuel eevee's armada. They now support Advanced Corporations, or so it seems. Hipyr has some of the greatest minds in the industry and even though the organization's size itself may be underwhelming their strange tech has proven a great threat against those who seek to uncover them.

Kal Agency

Kal Agency, one of the three rivaling factions mostly consisting of Antyshoal. Unlike the others Kal Agency does not use ENG stims, instead, their field "medics" use pages from spellbooks to repair battle damage. Their use of orange magic has proven devastating in war and impressive in industry.

Knights of Kerauno

The Knights of Kerauno is a Veodian faction. Mostly unheard of but rising in power quickly, Veodians are still quite new to Zeron but their focus on star core based tech has created unique powerful technologies and sonic weapons. Since star cores are so rare most could not sustain such tech, but the Knights of Kerauno are based of a galaxy on the rim of Zeron, where star cores are more common.

Maltech Vestus

MV or Maltech Vestus, one of the three rivaling factions. MV is a conglomeration of different races, magic users and tech alike the only way to recognize members are their welding mask like helmets, yet that is not a sure sign.

MAGIC

Red

Text based / Needs: blood into rhunes / Affects: rage / Use: empower, activations, potions. Red magic is arguably the most powerful of the seven, it's uses are vast and non-limiting, however it is the most researched. Aeri are the users of red magic.

Orange

Material based / Needs: rhune text into flesh pages/books / Affects: greed / Use: prosthetic limbs, creatures and constructs. Used by Antyshoal, it comes in handy on the battlefield but it can be used for sabotage and infiltration.

Yellow

Material based / Needs: tissue into rhunes / Affects: sickness / Use: healing/biotech. The best healing magic, yellow magic is used by the Fayrish. Capable of healing sickness and disease but not able to repair much tissue. It is performed with a Rhune etched stamp and bio-organic paste, often applied to the back and leaving a "scar" that reflects the Rhune and the size of the stamp.

Blue

Auditory based / Needs: sound from rhune music / Affects: disconnection of mind and body / Use: hypnosis and illusions. Used by the Silmari, it is often considered a more powerful version of indigo. It is performed with instruments, which can be as small as a pair of chimes to a grand organ. Blue magic can even be cast from a music box, meaning complex deadly traps and spells can be triggered with a simple tripwire.

Indigo

Auditory based / Needs: water and voice / Affects: change of culture / Use: disguises. Used by both Veodians and Silmari, indigo magic is used for disguising oneself and altering appearances, which ranges from small additions to appearing as an object such as a box or even invisibly. It requires a spoken code to maintain and uses a water sphere to contain the figure and alter light sources. Note the disguises do not transform the physical figure in any way.

Violet

Text based / Needs: banner with rhunes / Affects: doubt, anxiety / Use: speed, power, agility. Rarely used, the Veodians are the main users of violet magic. Notably used on their fearsome war mechs giving them incomprehensible speed for something their size. It is written on banners, capes, tapestries, etc. The strength of the spell is based of the power of the rhune script and integrity of banner.

TECH

ENG/Crystals

"Energy" or ENG is a strange substance made from giant crystals commonly found in vast caves on moons and deep under some planets. It's properties make it suitable for a weapon propellent, fuel for both land vehicles and starcraft, explosives, it can harden metal alloys to a great degree and can be converted into power without much loss, it can even be used as a stimulate (although not without great side affects). However it comes at a cost, as it saps magic users greatly for unknown reasons.

Star cores

In the depths of space there are orbs of powerful energy, that differs from the energy found in the crystals. Star cores come in a few sizes and colors: White when very small, light blue, the blue and then a dark blue followed by black when they implode. Since they last for thousands of years the implosions are not much of an issue. They power the star frigates, but can be used to power machinery and even weapons, since they are so rare it is not feasible for most to use them this way.

Powder and Gas

Gunpowder and Gas are rarer substances in Zeron, not because they are hard to make, but since the ENG mines and factories were founded few try to make these lesser used (and less efficient) materials. Magic users do use powder weapons, most notably the Antyshoal field medics.

RACES

Fayrish

Bird/moth-like “fairies” that use bio-tech and wear mask similar to plague doctors. Despite being skilled at healing with yellow magic, all are born ill with an incurable disease.

Aeri

Giant wolves wielding giant swords. Primitive and tribal but there are a few in smaller settlements. Aeri strike fear into even the fiercest warriors. Their size and strength along with red magic make them near unstoppable in the heat of battle.

Silmari

Aquatic but able to breath on land. Silmari have shells for heads that they pass down for generations, the shells grow larger and the spikes on the top that finally mature into a full circle. They have steampunk tech powered from underwater vents. They use blue and indigo magic.

Yarian

Lizard folk. Very susceptible to diseases. Xenophobic, they prefer to stay away From all other races other than Fayrish. They have large castles and medieval weapons, occupying many large planets away from the core. Although Yarian may seem to have a low constitution and are weak physically, they make up for it by being squishy and rubbery. They are incredibly hard to kill in combat and often come back from deathly hits many times.

Eiraditch

Human like apart from digitigrade legs and odd gemlike eyes, Eiraditch are almost indestructible and are huge in numbers. The Eiraditch are war driven and their culture revolves around it, but are still recovering from the last. They only use meele polearms, a ranged weapon in the hands of an Eiraditch is shameful.

Antyshoal

Black fur and canine-like. In the top three most common races. They wear strange white masks and never expose there faces. Antyshoal have great libraries of spellbooks, records and literature alike.

Veodian

Cyborg lizards. They are rising in power but still little in number, not much is known of them by other races. They have star core tech along with sonic weapons. They use violet and indigo magic.

Coromoth

Triangle headed “robots” created by the Eiraditch for a war. Heard to have organic wiring, and much more sentient than any other robot thus why they are deemed a race. Coromoth are hive minded, they have the capacity to make individual decisions but all interconnected through a network.

Human

Somehow found there way into Zeron, now they are one of the most common races, and by far the most common in cities. Most humans in Zeron focus on tech, but there are a few who study magic.
